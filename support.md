## Common questions

**Why is my route going through a parking lot or odd place?**
RouteCraft uses OpenStreetMap data via OpenRouteService to plan routes. Sometimes the algorithm chooses paths that are technically passable but feel strange. Tap "Regenerate" for a different option.

**The wind direction feature isn't matching what I expect.**
Wind data comes from OpenWeatherMap and updates roughly every 10 minutes. If wind is calm (under 5 km/h) the direction won't matter much.

**My route is the wrong length.**
RouteCraft generates routes within your requested range, but for some start points the road network can't quite hit the exact length you asked for. The app shows the closest match it could find. Try a different distance or start point.

**How do I export to Strava or Garmin?**
Tap any saved route → "Save & Export GPX" → choose your destination app. Most fitness apps accept GPX files.
